Solo mode

A mindful little adventure that trains your eye

Everything in solo mode stays on your device — no account, no internet needed. Capture with your camera or pull from your gallery, drag to rearrange the grid, and pick a frame style: clean contact-sheet lines, rounded color-pop gaps, or a classic polaroid look. Save to your gallery and share anywhere.

  • Free — every grid size and frame style unlocked
  • No ads, no account needed to play solo
  • Solo mode is fully on-device and offline
  • Live group hunts — share a 6-letter code, no accounts
  • Vote mode: everyone picks the best shot per cell
  • Room map — pin your favorite shots, opt-in
  • Shareable final-standings podium card
  • Three frame styles: contact, color pop, polaroid
  • Beautiful, shareable results — privacy-first by design

Group mode

A live color scavenger hunt with friends

Turn any color into a party. Create a room, and ColorCollect hands you a 6-letter code — friends join with the code or an invite link, no accounts, just a name. Everyone hunts the same color in real time and every shot lands on the shared grid live. In Vote mode you all pick the best photo for each cell, then one montage is built from everyone's winning shots. The finish line is a shareable final-standings card — gold, silver, and bronze, with the join code baked in, so every share doubles as an invite.

Miles apart? ColorCollect also works as a game for long-distance friends — and it doubles as a full photo scavenger hunt app for parties and team days.

Room map

Pin your best finds on the room map

Every group hunt comes with a shared live map. Snap a great match and — if you want to — drop it on the room's map, so everyone can see where the color was hiding. Pins pop up for the whole room in real time, turning your hunt into a little story of the neighborhood. And it's entirely up to you: nothing lands on the map unless you put it there.
