Where to find pink in the wild

Pink is red with the volume turned down — playful, warm, and everywhere once you start looking: blossoms, frosting, sunsets, neon. It's the color cities use when they want to be charming.

Spotting ideas for a pink scavenger hunt:

  • Cherry or almond blossoms
  • A frosted doughnut or macaron in a bakery window
  • A pink bicycle or helmet
  • Sunset clouds
  • Flamingo motifs on clothes or walls
  • A rose garden or flower stall
